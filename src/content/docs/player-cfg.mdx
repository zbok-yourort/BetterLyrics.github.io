title: Player Configuration
---

Usage: Find your music player and install the `Plugin` listed in the table.

> Supported music players include, but are not limited to, the list below. Please refer to your actual experience for confirmation.

> All media players that have integrated with [SMTC](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/uwp/audio-video-camera/integrate-with-systemmediatransportcontrols) are supported.
