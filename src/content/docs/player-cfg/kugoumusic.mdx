> [KuGou](https://www.kugou.com) (Chinese: 酷狗音乐; pinyin: KùGǒu Yīnyuè; lit. 'Cool Dog Music') is a Chinese music streaming and download service established in 2004 and owned by Tencent Music.[^1]

## Setup Guide

The official client has **timeline** synchronization issues.
We recommend using the third-party client [MoeKoe Music](https://github.com/MoeKoeMusic/MoeKoeMusic) for a better experience.

[^1]: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KuGou)
