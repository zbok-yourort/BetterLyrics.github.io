> [QQ Music](https://y.qq.com) (Chinese: QQ音乐) is one of three Chinese freemium music streaming services owned by Tencent Music. As of 2018, the service is set to reach over 700 million users with an estimated 120 million subscribers.[^1]

## Setup Guide

### Basic Setup

Please use a newer version of the client and enable **Show System Media Transport Controls (SMTC)** in the settings.

### Precise Matching

Please install the [QQMusic-ID-Injector plugin](https://github.com/apoint123/QQMusic-ID-Injector) and place **QQ Music** at the top of the lyrics source list.
